I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Cambridge, working with Dr. [Sarah Teichmann](https://www.teichlab.org/). I received my PhD from the Center for Bioinformatics (CBI), Peking University, under the supervision of Dr. [Ge Gao](https://www.gao-lab.org/index.php/people-gegao-2/). Please see my CV ([PDF](https://xiachenrui.github.io/assets/pdf/XiaCR-CV-2025.pdf)) for more information.

My research focuses on developing computational methods to decipher omics data. Recently, I have been particularly interested in large-scale spatial omics data modeling, including niche annotation, multi-slices integration, and representation learning.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing badminton.
