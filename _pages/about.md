I am fifth year PhD student at the Center of Bioinfomatics (CBI) at Peking University supervised by Dr. [Ge Gao](https://www.gao-lab.org/index.php/people-gegao-2/). Please see my CV ([PDF](https://xiachenrui.github.io/assets/pdf/XiaCR-CV-2025.pdf)) for more information.

My research interest is developing new computational tools to decipher omics data. Recently I focus on large scale spatial omics data modeling & integration, including multi-slices integration, spatial-awared embedding.
